Queue for You

Have you ever finished a really captivating television show and were left wondering “what show should I watch next?” Instead of going straight to the internet and typing that question into the search bar every single time, let’s make your new show-finding experience a little more entertaining, plethoric, and most importantly: tailored to you.

Make your queue now!

Instructions

  1. Answer some questions about your interests.
  2. As shows come up on cards, press either the thumbs up button (interested in watching), thumbs down button (not interested in watching), or the - button (already watched).
  3. Some show cards will have a gold star on them, which means they are shows I watched and recommend!
  4. When done, press "Get My List" to see which shows you want to watch, along with the option to download.
